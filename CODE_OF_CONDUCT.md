# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Prioritize collaboration and clarity.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that is reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

## Reporting
Report conduct issues to:
- `conduct@unoforge.dev`

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
